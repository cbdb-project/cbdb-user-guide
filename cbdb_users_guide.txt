The China Biographical Database (CBDB) is a relational database of biographical

experience of oпђГce and so on. The various types of information about the

CBDB also supports a second approach to analyzing the lives of large numbers of

fact that a person can move to many diпђАerent places. This is called a вАЬone-to-manyвАЭ

LookAtNetworks is the most powerful querying interface developed for the Access version

with a list of people, then all the people on that list serve as starting points. If one starts with a
place or list of places, then the people initially identiпђБed as associated with that place or those
places serve as the starting points.
